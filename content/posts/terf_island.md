[Lights Out for TERF Island](https://wtrem.bandcamp.com/album/lights-out-for-terf-island) is the seventh [w/trem](http://wtrem.bandcamp.com) album proper, and a departure from the folky/shoegazey template followed by previous recordings. It has [string arrangements](https://youtu.be/Wm-JnPOSxlo), [glamrock guitar riffs](https://youtu.be/hk0po6xhJJ8), [throbbing synths](https://youtu.be/CDV_L9nfVu8), [spoken word sections](https://youtu.be/CDV_L9nfVu8) and [lyrics about the UK being nuked by its European neighbours in a desperate attempt to contain a virulent moral panic](https://youtu.be/_cjjDMD7vyk). You can hear it on [Bandcamp](https://wtrem.bandcamp.com/album/lights-out-for-terf-island), [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/album/0RziKHUEnOdSq8jHSW6nnI?si=jG00sxPdSHeYsyrU4ejiNw), and [YouTube](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=OLAK5uy_nU4fsrctH4KGtCuJUZSlJPt4irF0-6Mrw).
